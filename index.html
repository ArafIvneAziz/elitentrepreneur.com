    What we do for our clients:


    Irresistible offer:
    We build our clients an irresistible offer…

    Because when you use setters to do outreach you need people interested as soon as they hear your offer.

    Creating an irresistible offer is extremely easy. You don't need to go crazy and promise everything.

    The formula of creating an offer is simple outcome, timeframe, and pain avoidance (optional)


    Creating the Ai system + content assets:
    You also need a System that controls the consistency of sending dms and converting the attention into booked qualified meetings.

    The role of the content assets(VSL, landing page, profile contents etc) is to educate the client on what you offer and how you can get them their desired outcome.

    leverage client results, give away free value, have good content, a DM CTA in bio that relates to what you do with.

    While your Main VSL should educate them on a bird's eye view of your services and program.

    In this VSL you can also mention things such as client results you have gotten.

    Most importantly in the Content assets you want to walk the client through all the stages of your service/ program.

    This way they have a clear image of what they can expect.

    Now that you have an irresistible offer and consistent system and landing page and VSL,content assets to compliment that offer, what you need is setters that can handle the conversation on your behalf.


    Hiring:
    Now what we did for our clients is we hired anywhere from 2/3 expert outbound appointment setters.

    Their role is to reach out to the exact perfect target audience of our clients and had a conversation with them.

    • Experience

    • Mindset

    • Future plans
